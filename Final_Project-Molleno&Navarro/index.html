About Us:

At K&P Visual Photography, we believe every story deserves to be told beautifully. Based in the Philippines, we offer affordable and professional photography and videography services for weddings, birthdays, events, and special occasions. K&P Visual started with a shared passion for storytelling through the lens. With years of experience and a creative eye, we set out to make professional photography accessible without compromising quality. Whether it’s a once-in-a-lifetime wedding or a simple family gathering, our mission is to capture moments that matter. We value authenticity, emotion, and connection. That’s why our style leans into natural light, real expressions, and unposed beauty—so your memories feel just as real years from now as they did in the moment. Every frame we take is crafted with care and heart.

Intramurals 2025

12 Mar 2025

Capturing the Spirit of CSS Intramurals 2025: Through My Lens by Kristoferson S. Navarro:
The CSS Department's intramurals this year were nothing short of unforgettable — and I had the amazing opportunity to capture it all through my lens as the official photographer. From the energy-filled performances of DANCEPA to the dazzling confidence of our Mr. and Ms. CSS candidates, every moment felt alive. I snapped dozens of shots, each one telling a different story of pride, preparation, and passion. The spotlight didn’t stop there — I also caught the crowd-favorite karaoke sessions, where voices soared (and sometimes cracked!) but spirits stayed high. It was wholesome, chaotic fun that brought everyone together. One of my favorite moments was photographing the volleyball team's practice. Their determination and team spirit were inspiring to witness. I loved how students reacted to the camera — some posed instantly, others smiled shyly, but most were just excited to be part of the moment. It reminded me how photography connects people. I also made sure to cover the NGE Building, where Esports battles kept the energy up for three days straight. The competition was intense, but Team LEO absolutely dominated the scene — showing skill, strategy, and pure focus. Overall, being the photographer gave me a front-row seat to all the action and allowed me to see how vibrant, talented, and fun the CSS community truly is. I’m proud to have frozen these moments in time — not just as a student, but as a storyteller through photos.
